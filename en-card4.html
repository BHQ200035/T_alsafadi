Opening a Branch for an Existing Company

Successfully expanding your business in the Saudi market

The Saudi market is one of the largest and fastest-growing markets in the Middle East, making the opening of a branch for an existing company a strategic step towards expansion and growth. However, the process of opening a branch can seem complicated due to the variety of laws and requirements that differ based on the company type and size

Steps for Opening a Foreign Company Branch in Saudi Arabia

Opening a branch for a foreign company requires a set of legal and administrative procedures. These steps aim to officially register the company and obtain all necessary licenses to begin commercial activity

  • Obtaining a License from the Ministry of Investment
    The license is necessary to enable the company to operate legally in Saudi Arabia based on the type of commercial activity
  • Signing the Company's Articles of Association
    The company's articles of association must be signed and authenticated by the Saudi Embassy to ensure legal recognition of the company in the Kingdom
  • Issuing the Commercial Register
    Requires submitting the parent company's commercial register and the partners' approval to open a branch in Saudi Arabia
  • Opening Company Files Registering the company with authorities such as the Zakat Authority, the Ministry of Labor, and Social Insurance
  • Obtaining the General Manager's Visa Obtaining the branch manager's visa to follow up on legal and administrative matters in Saudi Arabia

Required Documents for Opening a Foreign Company Branch

The process of opening a branch for a foreign company requires a set of official documents that prove the identity of the parent company and its financial and administrative capacity to manage a branch in Saudi Arabia

Submission of the Parent Company's Commercial Register after Authentication

Commercial Name Reservation with the Saudi Ministry of Commerce

Company's Articles of Association or Bylaws, depending on its type

Data of the Founding Partners and Capital

Resolution appointing a General Manager for the Branch with proof of identity

Additional Approvals depending on the nature of the commercial activity
